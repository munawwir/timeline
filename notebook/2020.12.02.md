5 GF mice were harvested (all male, DOB 11/23/20). Tissues snap frozen and stored at -80:

- Liver
- Gallbladder
- Fat
    - Gonadal
    - iWAT
    - iBAT
- Intestinal contents + Mucosa
    - Duodenum
    - Jejunum
    - Ileum
    - Cecum
    - Proximal colon
    - Distal colon
- Gastrocnemius muscle
