### Abu Lahab
#### 549 - 624

He was the uncle of Rasulullah ﷺ and a great enemy of Islam. He is condemned in Surah al-Massad.

>  تَبَّتْ يَدَآ أَبِى لَهَبٍ وَتَبَّ

> Perish the two hands of Abū Lahab, and perish he!  
> — Mufti Taqi Usmani
