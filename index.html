What is this site?

This is a Seerah "timeline." It's not a timeline in a strict sense, but more like an interactive network of people and events mentioned in Shaykh AbÅ«l Hasan AlÄ« NadwÄ«'s Prophet of Mercy. The timeline of events is here, where major events based on book chapter headings are listed from earliest to latest date. I use Gregorian calendar dates for everything for now. I intended to use Hijri dates as well, but I need to write some python stuff for that and I prioritized the concept of the entire site over details such as that. Once I finish all the scripting for core parts of the site, than inshaAllah I can figure out how to add Hijri dates. A lot of this is incomplete. You could help complete it by contributing typed notes for Seerah by either a) sending them to me or b) uploading them straight to the GitHub repo (which is the easier route for me, if you are familiar with GH or markdown). If you're even more ambitious, you could help with linking people and events through relative links in markdown. Also, some info on the word art on the home page: This is a word cloud. What it basically is, is I took all the words from the book, figured out which words are the most frequent (i.e. how many times that word was said), then turned it into an image where the bigger the word it, the more times it was mentioned. If you hover over a word, it will give you the exact number.
